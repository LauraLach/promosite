Hamilton the Musical

Hamilton is back!

Hamilton, the award-winning Broadway musical, is back after being closed because of COVID! Hamilton is a musical about Alexander Hamilton, a Founding Father of the United States of America. The musical features many catchy hip-hop songs and a variety of choreography styles. It has won a large number of awards, including Tony, Grammy, and Pulitzer awards. In New York City, the musical is performed at Richard Rodgers Theatre on W 46th Street.
